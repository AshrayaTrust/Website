Get Involved

Your time and support are invaluable to our mission.

Volunteer With Us

Become a part of the Ashraya family and experience the joy of selfless service. We welcome volunteers for various activities, especially during events like Ugadi Seva.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a direct impact on the lives of the needy.
  • Be part of a compassionate and dedicated community.
  • Experience the fulfillment of Seva (selfless service).
  • Contribute during special events like Ugadi Yatri Seva.

Current Opportunities

Teaching support on Sundays, help with food packing, medical professionals for camps, event support for Ugadi Seva.

"Volunteering at Ashraya changed my perspective. It's more than charity; it's connecting with humanity." - [Vishnu Kaza]
