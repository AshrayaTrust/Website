Donate Goods (In-Kind)

Beyond monetary support, your contribution of essential goods directly aids our beneficiaries. We gratefully accept new or gently used items in good condition.

Items We Often Need:
  • Rice, Dal, Cooking Oil, Vegetables
  • Wheat Flour (Atta), Sugar, Salt
  • Spices (Turmeric, Chilli powder etc.)
  • Tea Powder
  • Notebooks, Pens, Pencils
  • School Bags, Geometry Boxes
  • Blankets (Clean, good condition)
  • New/Gently Used Clothing (Clean)

Please ensure food items are within their expiry date and all items are clean and functional.

Due to storage limitations and specific needs, please contact us before bringing donations to arrange a suitable time and confirm requirements.
