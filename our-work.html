Our Work & Initiatives

Touching lives through compassionate service.

Serving the Needy: Our Core Activities

Since 2011, Ashraya Trust, under the guidance of Avadhoota Nadananda, has been dedicated to uplifting the lives of the underprivileged in and around Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh. Our work focuses on providing essential necessities and fostering hope through various seva activities.

Kshuda Nivarana

Ensuring no one sleeps hungry is a core tenet of Ashraya Trust. Our Kshuda Nivarana program provides daily, nutritious meals to those most in need.

Previous Next

Daily Food Distribution

Every single day, our dedicated team of volunteers prepares and distributes around 700-750 fresh food packets.

  • Beneficiaries: Patients' attendants (General Hospital), travelers (bus stand, railway station), devotees, Leprosy Colony residents, roadside dwellers.
  • Provides crucial sustenance and a moment of respite.
  • Community-driven: Individuals often sponsor distribution for special occasions.

Impact: Daily relief from hunger for hundreds, ensuring basic nutritional needs are met.

Rugna Nivarana

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We strive to provide free medical services.

Regular Medical Services

  • Weekly Camps at Ashraya: Free diagnosis, treatment, medication every Sunday.
  • Rural Outreach (Mandlem): Camps every alternate Sunday.
  • Mobile Dispensary: Reaching remote areas.
  • Eye Care: Occasional camps with free medication/surgery facilitation.

Impact: Improved access to primary healthcare, early diagnosis, prevention, and eye care.

AksharaTara Gurukulam

A visionary project reviving the ancient Gurukula system, blending tradition with modern education.

Concept and Features

  • Located in Mandlem village.
  • Unique 15-year residential program from age 5.
  • Nurturing inherent aptitude and passion.
  • Personalized curriculum.
  • Integrates learning of the 64 ancient Indian Kalas.
  • Joyous learning, life skills, character building.

Impact: Cultivating well-rounded, skilled, ethically grounded individuals connected to their heritage.

Ugadi Yatri Seva

A monumental annual effort serving thousands of pilgrims walking to Srisailam during Ugadi.

Previous Next

Recognizing the immense physical strain, we provide comprehensive care:

Seva Activities

  • Sacred Oil Massage: Using Guruji's special Siddha oil.
  • 24/7 Food & Refreshments: Hot meals, buttermilk, snacks.
  • Wound Care: Cleaning and dressing foot wounds.
  • Medical Support: Round-the-clock doctors.
"We consider this not just service, but 'Pooja to the walking Shivas'..." - Inspired by Guruji's teachings

Impact: Easing hardships for thousands, enabling pilgrims to complete their spiritual journey with dignity.
